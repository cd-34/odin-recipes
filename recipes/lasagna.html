Lasagna

Description

Yummy lasagna very tasty

Ingredients

  • 450g Lasagna sheets
  • 450g Lean ground beef
  • 450g Tomatoes
  • 450g Parmesan cheese
  • 1 Large onion

Steps

  1. Cook beef, onions, and tomatoes in seasoning
  2. Boil the lasagna sheets
  3. Get a large tray and put down a layer of the tomato and beef mixture
  4. Add a layer of the lasagna sheets on top and repeat
  5. Add a layer of cheese on top
  6. Cook in oven at 450 degrees celsius until done
