Burrito

Description

Yummy burrito very healthy

Ingredients

  • 1 Tortilla
  • 450g Black beans
  • 450g Extra lean ground beef
  • 450g Bell peppers
  • 20g Taco seasoning

Steps

  1. Cook beef with salt and taco seasoning
  2. Cook beans and bell peppers with salt
  3. Put everything into a bowl
  4. Heat up tortilla
  5. Throw all ingredients into the tortilla and wrap
