Chicken Soup

Description

Yummy chicken soup very healthy

Ingredients

  • 1L Chicken stock
  • 1 Costco rotisserie chicken
  • 450g Onion
  • 450g Red kidney beans
  • 5 Chipotle peppers in adobo

Steps

  1. Tear apart the chicken
  2. Cook beans in stock and add salt
  3. Cook onions and toss them into the stock
  4. Throw in chipotle peppers and chicken
  5. Blend to desired consistency and serve
